# FlowDesk Password Reset Troubleshooting

## Scenario

A customer is unable to reset their FlowDesk account password or reports that the password reset process is not working.

---

## Symptoms

- Password reset email is not received.
- Reset link has expired.
- Reset link displays an error.
- Customer receives an "Invalid Token" message.
- Password reset completes but login still fails.

---

## Troubleshooting Steps

### Step 1: Verify the Email Address

Confirm that the customer is using the email address associated with their FlowDesk account.

---

### Step 2: Check Spam or Junk Folder

Ask the customer to check their Spam, Junk, or Promotions folder for the password reset email.

---

### Step 3: Request a New Password Reset Link

Generate a new password reset request and ensure the customer uses the latest email received.

---

### Step 4: Verify Link Expiration

Inform the customer that password reset links expire after a certain period for security reasons. If expired, request a new link.

---

### Step 5: Test in Another Browser

Ask the customer to open the reset link in:

- Incognito/Private mode
- Another web browser
- Another device

---

### Step 6: Confirm Service Status

Check whether the authentication or email service is experiencing an outage.

---

## Escalation Criteria

Escalate the issue if:

- Multiple customers report the same problem.
- Password reset emails are not being generated.
- Authentication service is unavailable.
- The issue cannot be reproduced after completing all troubleshooting steps.

---

## Resolution

Document:

- Root cause
- Steps taken
- Final resolution
- Whether the issue was escalated
- Time taken to resolve
