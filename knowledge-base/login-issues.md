# FlowDesk Login Issues

## Issue

A customer reports that they cannot log in to their FlowDesk account.

---

## Possible Causes

- Incorrect email address
- Incorrect password
- Expired password
- Browser cache or cookies
- Internet connection issues
- Account temporarily locked after multiple failed login attempts
- Server outage or authentication service disruption

---

## Troubleshooting Steps

### Step 1

Confirm the customer is using the correct email address.

---

### Step 2

Ask the customer to reset their password using the **Forgot Password** option.

---

### Step 3

Ask the customer to clear their browser cache and cookies before attempting to log in again.

---

### Step 4

Try logging in using:

- Incognito Mode
- Another browser
- Another device

---

### Step 5

Check the FlowDesk Status Page for any ongoing service outages.

---

### Step 6

If the issue persists, verify whether the customer's account has been locked due to multiple failed login attempts.

---

## Escalation Criteria

Escalate the issue to the Engineering Team if:

- Password reset fails repeatedly.
- Multiple users report the same issue.
- Authentication service is unavailable.
- Login succeeds internally but fails for customers.

---

## Resolution

Document:

- Root cause
- Troubleshooting steps performed
- Final resolution
- Time to resolve
- Whether escalation was required
