# SaaS Product Support Portfolio

## Overview

Welcome to my Product Support Portfolio.

This repository showcases my practical understanding of Product Support and Technical Support through realistic SaaS support scenarios.

The portfolio is built around **FlowDesk**, a fictional cloud-based project management platform, to demonstrate how Product Support teams troubleshoot customer issues, document solutions, manage support requests, and deliver excellent customer experiences.

The goal of this portfolio is to reflect the daily responsibilities of a Product Support Specialist in a modern SaaS environment while continuously expanding my technical support knowledge.

---

# About FlowDesk

**FlowDesk** is a fictional Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform that enables teams to collaborate, manage projects, assign tasks, share files, communicate, and track project progress.

### Core Features

- User Authentication
- Team Collaboration
- Task Management
- Project Dashboard
- File Uploads
- Notifications
- Billing & Subscription Management
- Reports & Analytics
